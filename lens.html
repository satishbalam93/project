ABOUT US

Lenskart is an Indian multinational optical prescription eyewear retail chain, based Gurugram.As of March 2023, Lenskart has more than 2,000 retail stores, three-fourths of which are in India. Its manufacturing facility in New Delhi manufactures 3 lakh glasses a month.
